IMPULSE

Impulse is a game where you can challenge yourself with different games(mostly IQ). The games include, connect the dots, memorize the pattern, grammar test, fill in the mathematical equation, or my personal favorite, candy sort. It's basically unshuffling beakers with different colours then placing them into another beaker with those same colours that you have transferred. Basically saying blue goes with blue and red goes with red.

5 Reasons why this game is my Favorite

  • It tests my IQ
  • It entartains me
  • It helps calm myself
  • Its free
  • It fills up my time
